Privacy Choices

This page explains how Mosaic users can manage permissions, consent choices, and privacy-related requests.

Privacy Policy Support

Manage permissions on iPhone

  • Open the iPhone Settings app.
  • Scroll to `Mosaic`.
  • Review and update Photo Library and Camera access as needed.

Advertising and consent choices

If Mosaic shows a privacy or consent options flow in your region, you can use the in-app privacy options entry point to review available advertising choices. The exact options shown may vary by jurisdiction and the services enabled in the current version of the app.

Subscriptions

To manage or cancel a subscription, open your Apple account subscription settings on your device.

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Mosaic handles information when people use the app to create collages, edit templates, import photos, and access premium features.

Effective date: May 29, 2026 Operator: Sweta Rahul Patel
Terms of Use Privacy Choices Support

1. What this policy covers

This Privacy Policy applies to the Mosaic mobile application and related support communications operated by Sweta Rahul Patel. It covers information collected directly by the app and information collected by third-party services integrated into the app, such as advertising and consent tools.

2. Information you provide through the app

Mosaic is designed primarily to work on-device.

  • Photos and images you choose from your photo library are used to build and edit collages inside the app.
  • Images you save are written back to your photo library only when you request that action.
  • If camera capture is enabled in the version you use, photos captured with the camera are used for collage creation inside the app.
  • If you contact support, we may receive the information you include in your message, such as your name, email address, and the contents of your request.

3. Information collected automatically

The app and its third-party SDKs may collect limited technical information automatically, including:

  • device and app information needed for ads, fraud prevention, or SDK operation
  • diagnostic or performance information generated by integrated SDKs
  • consent status and privacy choice state used to determine whether ads can be requested
  • local app state stored on-device, such as whether onboarding has already been shown

4. Advertising and consent

Mosaic currently integrates Google Mobile Ads and Google's User Messaging Platform. These services may collect or process identifiers, usage data, diagnostics, and consent-related information depending on your device, region, and the consent choices you make.

Where required, Mosaic shows a consent flow so users can review privacy choices before personalized advertising or certain ad requests are made.

5. Subscriptions and payments

Premium features may be offered through Apple in-app purchases. Billing, payment credentials, and subscription management are handled by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card details.

6. How we use information

  • to provide collage editing, template browsing, image import, save, and share features
  • to enable and manage ad-supported experiences
  • to record and apply consent choices
  • to respond to support requests
  • to maintain app security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot problems

7. How information is shared

We may share information in these limited situations:

  • with service providers and SDK partners that help operate app functionality, ads, or consent workflows
  • if required by law, legal process, or valid government request
  • to protect the rights, safety, or property of users, the public, or Sweta Rahul Patel
  • as part of a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to applicable law

8. Data retention

Mosaic is primarily an on-device app. User-selected images remain on your device unless you save, export, or otherwise share them. Support emails and related records may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to handle the request, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

9. Your choices

  • You can control photo library and camera access through iOS Settings.
  • You can manage advertising-related privacy choices through the in-app consent or privacy options flow where available.
  • You can cancel subscriptions through your Apple account subscription settings.
  • You can contact swetanandha93@gmail.com to request help regarding privacy questions.

10. Children's privacy

Mosaic is not directed to children under 13, and Sweta Rahul Patel does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through the app. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact swetanandha93@gmail.com.

11. International users

Depending on where you are located, your information may be processed by third-party providers in countries other than your own, subject to applicable law and their own data handling practices.
